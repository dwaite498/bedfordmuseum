Welcome to the Bedford Museum

We are proud to the be the custodians and providers of Bedford's history. Our exhibits include Early Native life through the War Between the Statesm and further into the Twentieth Century. Our mission is to develop and maintain files for genealogical and historical research as well as collect, preserve, and interpret object of historical significance to the Town of Bedford and Bedford County, Virginia.

Visit the Museum

We hope that you and your family are able to come and visit us in the near future. We have a selection of exciting exhibits and tour opportunities that show how Bedford County was at the center of the Westward expansion, Indian culture, and provided many brave sould for our national defense. Come and learn more about our history and be sure to ask the on staff genealogist about how you can leave knowing more about your own history as well.

Admission

The Museum and Genealogical Library is free and open to the public, however a $5.00 donation is suggested per adult.

Support Us

The Bedford museum cannot open its doors every day on its own. We need people like you to help us maintain and expand our programs and knowledge of the history of Bedford County. We gladly accept donations of all sizes and are always looking for volunteers to assist us in our work.
