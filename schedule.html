Museum Schedule

Monthly Genealogy Classes

Free Genealogy classes, presented by our on staff genealogist, on the first four Saturdays every month 10am-12pm.

  • First Saturday is "How to Get Started"
  • Second Saturday is "How to Use Census Records"
  • Third Saturday is "How to Find and Utilize Local Sources."
  • The Fourth Saturday is "How to Utilize the Internet in Genealogical Research."

There are also night classes available on alternating months. In February the classes will be on Saturday mornings. In March the classes will be on Tuesday nights 6pm-8pm.

Forum Schedule

The Forum is an informal group that that meets every month on second Saturday (Except December and January) to discuss interesting historical topics. The public is invited and we meet on the third floor of the Museum (Elevator Provided) at 1:30PM and the meetings usually last about 90 minutes.

  • June 3rd: Tour of Avenel. Meeting at Avenel at 1:15, tour starts at 1:30pm. Any questions give the museum a call at 540-586-4520.
  • July 9th: A behind the scenes tour of Jones Memorial at 2:00 PM
  • August 12th: Genealogy Panel at the Museum. Local representatives will be available for panel style questionnaire
  • September 9th: Karen Hall is coming to give a presentation on the CCC and their training camps here in Bedford.
  • October and November: No Forums
